As a physics tutor, I notice that the moment students hit Grade 10, they are absolutely terrified of Physical Sciences. It's a completely new subject for them, and society has conditioned everyone to believe it's this impossibly difficult monster. I spend my lessons trying to break down that fear and show them how logical and approachable it actually is.

That’s exactly why I built this portal. I wanted to make sure that once our tutoring sessions end, my students still have a dedicated lifeline at home. It gives them an interactive space completely tailored to their exact CAPS curriculum, keeping them aligned and up-to-date with what they are actively dealing with in class every single week.
